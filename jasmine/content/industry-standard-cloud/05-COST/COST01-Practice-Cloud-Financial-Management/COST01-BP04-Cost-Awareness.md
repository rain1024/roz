# COST01-BP04: Implement cost awareness in organizational processes

## Description

Build cost awareness culture trong organization.

## Implementation Guidance

- Include cost in architecture reviews
- Train engineers on cost optimization
- Show cost impact of decisions
- Gamify cost savings
- Recognize cost optimization efforts

## Risk Level

Medium - Low awareness leads to waste.
