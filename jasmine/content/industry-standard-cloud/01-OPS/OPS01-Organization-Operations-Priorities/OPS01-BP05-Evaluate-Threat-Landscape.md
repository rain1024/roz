# OPS01-BP05: Evaluate threat landscape

## Description

Đánh giá threats có thể ảnh hưởng đến workload.

## Implementation Guidance

- Conduct threat modeling exercises
- Monitor security advisories and CVEs
- Understand attack vectors relevant to your workload
- Regular security assessments
- Implement threat intelligence feeds

## Risk Level

High - Unknown threats can lead to security breaches.
